These are some of the most commonly asked questions I get over email. If you have a question, free to contact me.


1. How much do your charge for a website?

    This is the most common question I get and the one that cannot be easily answered. Building a site is much like building a house. How many bedrooms do you want? How many bathrooms? 1 or 2 car garage? A fireplace? There are so many variables to pricing a web build. The marketing of your business will be focused around your website. Expect a large chunk of your marketing budget to go to a web build. If you do not have the budget I suggest starting with a WIX site or something similair to get you started before upgrading later on.

2. What marketing materials should I have already before starting a site?

    You should have a standard marketing kit that consists of a logo, brand colors, some original pictures and a record of testimonials which could be from review sites like Google Places or Yelp. Additionally having some testimonials from larger more mainstream publications can help in establishing your brand.

3. Do you still do social media marketing?

    No. Unless you are looking to branch out into the free socials like Truth, Gab, Rumble, and many more, I no longer have an interest in marketing on the mainstream socials where I have experienced entirely too much censorship.

4. Do you still do email marketing?

    Yes. For the time being I still enjoy creating email content and building up a community through email. Its not just the oldest but the best way to market on the Net.
